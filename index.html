Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any boating experience?

No experience is required! All our rentals come with a professional captain who will handle the navigation and operation of the boat. You can sit back, relax, and enjoy the experience.

What is included in the rental price?

Our rental prices include the boat, a licensed captain, fuel for standard cruising, basic amenities, and safety equipment. Additional services like catering, premium beverages, or special route requests may incur extra charges.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (December-April) and for weekend rentals. For special events or holiday periods, booking 1-2 months ahead is advisable to secure your preferred vessel.

What happens in case of bad weather?

Your safety is our priority. If weather conditions are unsafe for boating, we will work with you to reschedule your trip for another day or provide a full refund. The captain makes the final decision regarding weather safety.

Can we bring our own food and drinks?

Yes, you are welcome to bring your own food and beverages. Most of our boats have refrigeration and storage space. We also offer catering services if you prefer to have everything prepared for you.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 7 days before the rental date receive a full refund. Cancellations within 3-7 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 3 days before the rental date are non-refundable unless due to unsafe weather conditions.
